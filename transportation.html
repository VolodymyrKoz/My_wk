Welcome to ArtWays Corp Flatbed Shipping

ArtWays Corp has redefined the landscape of nationwide flatbed shipping services since its establishment in 1991. With a commitment to excellence, ArtWays Corp presents an array of exceptional advantages, including highly competitive rates, unparalleled responsiveness, expedited transit times, and a robust cargo management system. Our customer-centric approach, meticulous attention to detail, and streamlined routing combine to offer a flawless and superior shipping encounter. Notably, every shipment is fortified with insurance coverage up to $100,000.

Our Specialized Flatbed Shipping Offerings

  • Cutting-edge Conestoga covers, providing an extra layer of protection for your cargo en route.
  • Step deck flatbeds, adeptly engineered to accommodate and safeguard tall loads.
  • Unique double drop flatbeds with an impressive capacity to lower to an 18” deck level, ushering the transportation of towering 12-foot loads without the need for specialized permits.
  • Revolutionary RGN (Removable Gooseneck) trailers, facilitating effortless loading and unloading of equipment.
  • Versatile trailer lengths of 48 and 53 feet, accommodating diverse shipping needs.
  • Advanced side kits, ensuring the utmost security for your valuable cargo.
  • Tailored Less than Load (LTL) options, providing flexibility for various load sizes.
  • Trustworthy Low Boy trailers, a symbol of reliability in heavy-duty shipping.
